Real UCSF cohort

Growth by IDH status · 298 patients

What the cohort shows

IDH status is the dominant driver of glioma behaviour. In this UCSF-LPTDG cohort, 96 IDH-wildtype gliomas grow at a median multiplier of 1.57 and 70% measurably grew between scans.

The 123 IDH-mutant gliomas are far more indolent — median multiplier 0.92, only 55% growing. The 3D model above is seeded from a real baseline and driven at these measured rates.

Calibrated growth model — reproduces measured behaviour, not an out-of-sample prediction.
